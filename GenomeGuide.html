Step 3 of 4 — Structure & confidence
🧊 How well do we know its shape?

Scientists use a score called pLDDT to measure how confident the AI model is about each part of the protein's shape. Think of it like GPS accuracy — some regions are pinpointed perfectly, others are fuzzy.
